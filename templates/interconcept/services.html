Our Services

Below are the services we render from Loans to Real Estate, we ensure quality assurance in all our job and monitor all project from beginning to completion

Loan & Business lending

Our loans provide funding for individuals. It is a timed loan obtainable at a 5% monthly interest rate. Our loan product is aimed at providing the funds for healthcare, educational purposes, automobile, salary loans or personal home loans. We have come to the aid of many Nigerians and clients, while displaying a level of understanding and empathy not usually found in the corporate world. Our packages though simple, sets up the client to be independent and liquid enough not to seek further loans in the near future.

Real estate management

The company offers her services to individuals, corporate organizations and institutions. We serve as realtors to our clients; we buy, sell, search, process C of O and property consultancy. We are also involved in facility and project management and development.

Investment

We offer rates on a tri-annual and biannual basis ranging from 25.3% - 39.5% spread over 6 packages. Our investments are insured up to the tune of 1 billion naira with Consolidated Hallmark Insurance Plc

Daily & Targeted Savings

This product enables the client to save on daily basis a certain amount for the number of days in the month. If the customer is able to save for 3 consecutive months without withdrawing, he/she will not be charged. It is a unique savings product designed for customers who engage in daily income generating activities. It is a way of making our client’s work count so they don’t live out of their pockets.
